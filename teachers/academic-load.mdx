---
title: "How to assign and remove teacher academic load entries"
description: "Learn how to assign and remove academic loads, linking a teacher to a specific subject within a section using the seccion_docente_materia join table."
---

An academic load is the assignment of a teacher to teach a specific subject (`materia`) in a specific section (`seccion`). Managing academic loads lets you define exactly who teaches what and where, giving the system the information it needs to connect teachers to attendance records, schedules, and other academic data.

## How assignments are stored

Academic loads are stored in the `seccion_docente_materia` join table, which links three entities:

| Column | References | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `docente_id` | `docentes.id` | The assigned teacher |
| `seccion_id` | `secciones.id_seccion` | The section where the subject is taught |
| `materia_id` | `materias.id_materia` | The subject being taught |

The table enforces a unique constraint on `(seccion_id, materia_id)`, which means each subject in a given section can only have one teacher assigned to it at a time.

<Note>
  If a subject already has a teacher assigned in a section, attempting to create a second assignment for the same section–subject pair will fail. The `asignarCargaDocente` action checks for an existing record first and returns an error message before attempting the database write.
</Note>

## Assign a load

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the assignment panel">
    On the `/gestion-docentes` page, locate the teacher you want to assign a load to and open their academic load section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a section and subject">
    Choose the target section from the available sections list and then choose the subject you want to assign. Both lists are loaded from `secciones` and `materias` respectively.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the assignment">
    Submit the form. The `asignarCargaDocente(docenteId, seccionId, materiaId)` server action checks whether the assignment already exists. If it does not, the new record is created in `seccion_docente_materia` and the page revalidates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Remove a load

To remove an existing academic load, locate the assignment in the teacher's load list and click the remove action. The `removerCarga(id)` action deletes the record from `seccion_docente_materia` by its primary key `id`.

<Warning>
  Removing an academic load is immediate and cannot be undone from the UI. The subject will no longer have an assigned teacher in that section until a new assignment is created.
</Warning>

## Multiple loads per teacher

A teacher can hold any number of academic loads across different sections and subjects. There is no limit on how many assignments a single teacher can have — the unique constraint only prevents the same section–subject pair from being assigned to more than one teacher.

## Tips

<Tip>
  Before assigning a load, verify that the subject exists in the selected section's study plan. Assigning a teacher to a subject that is not part of a section's curriculum may cause inconsistencies in reports and attendance records.
</Tip>
